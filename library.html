Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Can I check out library books?

    2. A: Yes! Go to the Follett/Destiny button on the wakeid portal. When you see a book you would like, click the hold button. Mrs. Vinson will get the book for you and will deliver them to your classroom (in person students) or will arrange for pickup (virtual students).

  2. When will I get my books?

    3. A: Books will be available for pickup or delivery the week after books are put on hold.

  3. Do we have access to ebooks?

    4. A: Yes! We actually have both ebooks and audio books. Go to the Follett/Destiny button on the wakeid portal. You can filter searches by book format.

  4. What if a book I want is already checked out?

    5. A: Click the hold button and the system will automatically put you next in line for that book.

  5. What safety procedures are in place due to the pandemic?

    6. A: Books are bagged in plastic and labeled with student name for contactless pickup.Also, returned books are quaranteened for 72 hours before reshelving.
